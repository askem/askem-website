About Us

Askem is a technology company that builds simple, powerful products for people and businesses.

We founded Askem to help solve one of the most common challenges that brands, agencies, and digital marketers face…a lack of measurement and a failure to account for the impact of brand lift in their digital marketing efforts. We believe in transparency of market insights and we believe in accessibility for everyone involved.

Askem is a revolutionary start-up that encourages you to challenge the status quo of your marketing efforts. We believe digital ad campaigns can be measured by more than just click through rates and fuzzy conversion metrics on your website. Digital marketing has become so heavily focused on ROI and has mainly ignored the brand building and awareness impact. Every business advertises, and we know everyone should understand how their advertising spends is impacting more than just conversions.

Our values

Do!

We are a team of doers; we don’t talk the talk, we walk the walk.
